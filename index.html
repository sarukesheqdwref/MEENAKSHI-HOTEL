About Us

Traditional Taste Since 1995

Hotel Meenakshi is a cozy vegetarian restaurant offering authentic South Indian meals prepared using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes. Located near the sacred Meenakshi Amman Temple, our restaurant welcomes pilgrims, families, tourists, and food lovers with delicious breakfast, lunch, dinner, sweets, snacks, and filter coffee. Every meal is served with traditional hospitality that reflects the culture of Madurai.
